Suzanne's Bestsellers

The Hunger Games:
It won several awards, including the 2009 Golden Duck Award for Young Adult Fiction, 2012 California Reader Medal, and was a 2008 Cybils Award finalist.

Catching Fire:
Catching Fire won several awards, including the 2010 Children's Choice Book Award for Teen Choice Book of the Year and the 210 Georgia Peach Book Award for Teen Readers. It was also a New York Times bestseller

Mockingjay:
It won the 2011 Children's Choice Book Award for Teen Choice Book of the Year and the 2012 California Young Reader Medal. It was also a New York Times bestseller

The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes:
It won the 2021 Geffen Award for Best Science Fiction Book It was also a New York Times bestseller.
